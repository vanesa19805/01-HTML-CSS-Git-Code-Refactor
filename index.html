BIO

Tame Impala is the psychedellic music project of Australian multi-instrumentalist KEVIN PARKER. As a touring act, Take Impala consists of Parker (guitar, vocals), Dominic Simper (guital, synthesiser), Jay Watson (synthesiser, vocals, guitar), Cam Avery (bass guitar, vocals) and Julien Barbagallo (drums, vocals).

The group started with their first EP in September 2008, follow by Sundown Syndrome in 2009. In 2010 the band debut album "Innerspeaker" was release in May of that year. The album resulted in a album tour, oppening for acts such as MGMT's American tour.

Lonerism was release in 2012 the 2nd debut album. Currents was release in 2015, an album that created alot more noise for the band. 2019 Slow Rush is the most recent creation that has gaind the band all the more new followings it deserves.
